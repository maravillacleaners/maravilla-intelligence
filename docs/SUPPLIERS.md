## 1. Getting Started

### Registration Workflow

The supplier registration process is a 5-step workflow designed to collect all necessary business and service information:

#### Step 1: Company Information
1. Visit https://suppliers.maravillacleaners.com/suppliers/register
2. Enter your company details:
   - **Legal Business Name** (as registered with government)
   - **Business Website** (optional, helpful for verification)
   - **Service Category** (select your primary service type)
3. Click "Next" to proceed

#### Step 2: Contact Details
4. Enter your contact information:
   - **Contact Person Name** (name of primary contact)
   - **Business Email Address** (this is your login email)
   - **Phone Number** (preferred contact method)
   - **Password** (must meet password requirements below)
   - **Confirm Password** (must match)
5. Click "Next" to proceed

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must contain uppercase letters (A-Z)
- Must contain lowercase letters (a-z)
- Must contain numbers (0-9)
- Example: `MyCompany123` or `Supplier2024`

#### Step 3: Services
6. Select your services:
   - Check all service categories you offer
   - Select preferred geographic counties/regions
   - Multiple selections allowed (expands your opportunities)
7. Specify your **Annual Capacity** (estimated annual revenue capacity in USD)
8. Click "Next" to proceed

#### Step 4: Documents
9. Upload required documentation:
   - **Insurance Certificate** (PDF, current coverage)
   - **Business Certifications** (MBE, WBE, 8(a), HUBZone if applicable)
   - **SAM.gov Documentation** (if you have SAM.gov registration)
   - **CAGE Code Verification** (if you have CAGE code)
   - Additional relevant files (optional)
10. Click "Next" to proceed

#### Step 5: Confirmation
11. Review all entered information:
    - Check accuracy of all fields
    - Review selected services and locations
    - Confirm password strength
12. Accept the Supplier Portal Terms of Service
13. Click "Create Account" to submit

#### Account Status & Approval
- **Status Created**: Account created with "Pending Review" status
- **Admin Review**: Maravilla team reviews your submission within 24 hours
- **Approval Email**: You receive notification email with approval or rejection
- **Next Login**: Upon approval, you can login and view opportunities

### Login

#### Accessing Your Account
1. Navigate to https://suppliers.maravillacleaners.com/suppliers/login
2. Enter your business email address (used during registration)
3. Enter your password (created during registration)
4. Click "Sign In"
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard

#### Troubleshooting Login Issues

**"Account Not Found"**
- Check that email matches your registration email exactly
- Account may still be pending approval (check email for approval notification)
- Contact support: suppliers@maravillacleaners.com

**"Incorrect Password"**
- Verify CAPS LOCK is off
- Ensure password is entered correctly (passwords are case-sensitive)
- Use "Forgot Password" link to reset if needed

**"Account Inactive"**
- Your account has been deactivated
- Contact support to reactivate

### Password Management

#### Changing Your Password
1. Login to your account
2. Click your company name in top-right corner
3. Select "Profile Settings"
4. Click "Change Password" section
5. Enter current password
6. Enter new password (must meet password requirements)
7. Confirm new password
8. Click "Update Password"
9. You will be logged out and must login with new password

#### Resetting a Forgotten Password
1. On login page, click "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your business email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for password reset link
5. Click link in email (expires in 24 hours)
6. Enter new password (must meet password requirements)
7. Confirm new password
8. Click "Reset Password"
9. Login with your new password

Click "Sort by" dropdown to reorder opportunities:
- **Deadline (Soonest First)** - Expires first at top
- **Contract Value (Highest First)** - Largest contracts first
- **Match Score (Best Matches First)** - Highest matches first
- **Recently Added** - Most recent matches first

#### Search
Use search bar to find specific opportunities:
- Type opportunity name or agency name
- Search is instant as you type
- Clear search to show all opportunities

### Understanding Match Scores

Your match score for each opportunity is calculated from four factors:

#### Factor 1: Service Category Match (60% weight)
- Your selected services align with opportunity requirements
- More services you offer = higher match score
- Example: Opportunity needs "janitorial" and you offer janitorial = 60% starting score

#### Factor 2: Geographic Location Match (20% weight)
- Opportunity location is in your preferred counties
- Matching preferred counties adds points
- Example: Opportunity in Hillsborough County and you prefer Hillsborough = +20 points

#### Factor 3: Certification Bonus (10% weight)
- You have relevant business certifications
- Available certifications:
  - MBE (Minority Business Enterprise)
  - WBE (Women Business Enterprise)
  - 8(a) (Small Business Administration 8(a) program)
  - HUBZone (Historically Underutilized Business Zone)
- Each applies = +10 bonus points

#### Factor 4: Capacity Verification (10% weight)
- Your annual capacity is sufficient for contract value
- Calculated as: (Contract Value / Annual Capacity) * 100
- Example: $500K contract and $5M capacity = high capacity match

### Applying to Opportunities

#### Quick Apply
1. Find opportunity card on Opportunities page
2. Click "Apply Now" button
3. Confirm application in popup dialog
4. Click "Submit Application"
5. See confirmation message and status changes to "Applied"

#### Application Details View
1. Click opportunity name to view full details
2. Review complete opportunity information:
   - Full description of work required
   - Detailed requirements and qualifications
   - Contact information for questions
   - Supporting documentation links
3. Scroll to bottom and click "Apply Now"
4. Confirm application
5. Submitted successfully!

#### What Happens After You Apply

**Timeline:**
- **Day 0**: You submit application (status: "Submitted")
- **Day 1**: Admin receives notification of your application
- **Days 1-3**: Admin reviews your application and profile fit
- **Day 3-5**: Status updates to either "Accepted" or "Rejected"
- **Day 5+**: If accepted, you receive next steps email

**Status Updates:**
- Submitted - Application received and queued for review
- Under Review - Admin is actively reviewing your fit
- Accepted - Your application was selected! Next steps coming
- Rejected - Not selected this round (may apply to future similar opportunities)
- Withdrawn - You cancelled your application

### Why Applications Get Rejected

Common reasons for rejection:

1. **Service Mismatch** - Your services don't align with opportunity needs
2. **Location Not Served** - Opportunity outside your preferred counties
3. **Capacity Insufficient** - Contract value exceeds your annual capacity
4. **Missing Certifications** - Opportunity requires specific certifications you lack
5. **Profile Incomplete** - Missing required documents or information
6. **Already Selected** - We selected another supplier for this opportunity

**Improving Future Acceptance:**
- Keep profile current with all services you offer
- Add geographic coverage to preferred counties
- Complete all document uploads
- Increase annual capacity if possible
- Add relevant business certifications

## 4. Applications

Your applications are the record of all opportunities you've submitted for and their status.

### View All Applications

#### Accessing Applications
1. Click "My Applications" on dashboard, or
2. Use main menu: Suppliers > Applications
3. See table of all your applications

#### Application Table Columns

**Opportunity Name**
- Name of the opportunity you applied for
- Click to view opportunity details

**Agency**
- Government agency managing the opportunity
- Helps identify opportunity source

**Application Status**
- Current status of your application (see statuses below)
- Color-coded for quick scanning:
  - Gray = Submitted
  - Blue = Under Review
  - Green = Accepted
  - Red = Rejected

**Contract Value**
- Total value of the opportunity in USD
- Helps prioritize important applications

**Application Date**
- Date you submitted your application (YYYY-MM-DD)
- Sorted newest first by default

**Response Date**
- Date your application was reviewed and status updated
- Blank if status is still "Submitted" or "Under Review"

**Actions**
- View Details button to see full opportunity information
- Withdraw button (only while status is "Submitted")

### Application Statuses Explained

#### Submitted
- Your application was just received
- Admin queued it for review
- Status in this phase: 0-24 hours typically
- No action needed from you

#### Under Review
- Admin is actively reviewing your application
- Checking your profile fit with opportunity
- Evaluating your experience and capacity
- Status in this phase: 1-3 days typically
- No action needed from you

#### Accepted
- Congratulations! Your application was selected
- Admin will send you next steps email
- Next steps may include:
  - Providing additional documentation
  - Discussing teaming/partnerships
  - Reviewing contract terms
  - Scheduling meeting with agency contact
- Check your email for follow-up instructions

#### Rejected
- Your application was not selected
- May reapply to similar future opportunities
- Check rejection notes (if provided) to understand why
- Update your profile to improve future chances

#### Withdrawn
- You cancelled your application
- Status is final - cannot reactivate
- Can apply to same opportunity again if it remains available

### Filtering and Searching Applications

#### Filter by Status
Click "Filter by Status" to show:
- All Statuses (default - shows all)
- Submitted
- Under Review
- Accepted
- Rejected
- Withdrawn

#### Search by Opportunity
Use search box to find applications by:
- Opportunity name
- Agency name
- Contract value
- Search is instant as you type

#### Sort Order
Default sort: newest applications first
Click column header to sort by:
- Application Date (ascending/descending)
- Contract Value (high to low)
- Status (all statuses A-Z)

### What to Do When Accepted

When your application status changes to "Accepted":

1. **Check Your Email** (immediately)
   - Look for email from suppliers@maravillacleaners.com
   - Subject line includes opportunity name
   - Contains next steps specific to this opportunity

2. **Read Next Steps Carefully**
   - May be asked to provide additional information
   - May need to confirm your interest
   - May need to upload additional documents
   - May schedule meeting/call

3. **Respond Promptly** (within 24-48 hours)
   - Follow instructions in email exactly
   - Respond to any questions asked
   - Provide requested documents timely
   - Confirm your availability for next steps

4. **Provide Information**
   - Additional team member details
   - References or past work examples
   - Proof of bonding/insurance if requested
   - DUNS number or SAM.gov registration

5. **Keep Contact Details Current**
   - Ensure phone and email still valid
   - Check spam folder for our emails
   - Whitelist noreply@maravillacleaners.com

### Withdrawing an Application

#### When to Withdraw
You may withdraw your application if:
- You're no longer interested in the opportunity
- You've been selected by another opportunity
- Your circumstances have changed
- You realize you cannot fulfill requirements

#### How to Withdraw
1. Find application in your applications list
2. Click "..." (more options) button
3. Select "Withdraw Application"
4. Confirm in popup dialog: "Yes, withdraw"
5. Status changes to "Withdrawn" immediately
6. Notification sent to admin

#### After Withdrawal
- Status is final - cannot reactivate withdrawn application
- You may apply to same opportunity again if still available
- No penalty for withdrawals
- Admin is notified of your withdrawal

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: How long does it take for my account to be approved?**
A: Usually within 24 hours of registration submission. Check your email for approval notice. If more than 24 hours, contact us at suppliers@maravillacleaners.com.

**Q: Can I change the email address associated with my account?**
A: Your registered email is your login email and cannot be changed via portal. To change it, contact admin at suppliers@maravillacleaners.com with your request and your legal company name. This helps prevent unauthorized access.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your email, and we'll send a password reset link. The link expires in 24 hours. If you don't see email, check spam folder.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts for different divisions of my company?**
A: No, one account per business entity. If you have multiple business entities (different legal names), you can register each separately. Contact support to clarify if needed.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support at suppliers@maravillacleaners.com with your company name and request account deletion. We can deactivate your account. A new account can always be created if circumstances change.

### Opportunities

**Q: How are opportunities matched to me?**
A: Automatic matching algorithm considers:
- Your selected services (60% weight)
- Your geographic coverage (20% weight)
- Your certifications (10% weight)
- Your annual capacity (10% weight)

Opportunities are matched daily. New matches appear within 1-24 hours.

**Q: Why don't I see any opportunities?**
A: Possible reasons:
1. Your account is still "Pending Review" - you'll see opportunities after approval
2. No opportunities currently match your services
3. Limited geographic coverage - expand counties in profile
4. Annual capacity is too low for current opportunities
5. Missing required certifications for available opportunities

**Q: Can I apply to the same opportunity multiple times?**

- Password history not stored

#### Authentication & Sessions
- JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication
- Tokens expire after 30 days
- Tokens valid only for your account
- Tokens invalidated if password changes
- Automatic logout after 30 minutes of inactivity

- All data transmitted over encrypted connection
- Database records encrypted at rest
- Sensitive data fields encrypted additionally
- No plaintext credit card, SSN, or password data stored
